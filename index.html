Carry is Live

Ajey Nagar, better known as Carry Minati, is a YouTuber and streamer from Faridabad, India. He is known for his comedic skits and reactions to various online topics on his channel CarryMinati. As of 9 August 2020, Nagar is the most subscribed individual creator on YouTube India with over 24 million subscribers on his channel CarryMinati. His other channel Carryislive is dedicated to gaming and live streams.

Dynamo Gaming

Aditya Deepak Sawant (born: April 18, 1996 [age 24]), better known online as Dynamo Gaming, is an Indian YouTube gaming creator who posts and live-streams PUBG and PUBG Mobile videos on YouTube. He is the most subscribed Indian gaming YouTuber.Aditya was born in Mumbai, Maharashtra in April 1996 to parents Deepak Sawant and Vaishali Sawant, and was raised along with his sister Aaradhya Sawant.
